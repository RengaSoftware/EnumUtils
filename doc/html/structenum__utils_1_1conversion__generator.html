Detailed Description

template<typename E, typename T>
struct enum_utils::conversion_generator< E, T >

This functor is used to convert each item of enum E to a value of type T at runtime.

You can provide your specializations of the class as an alternate way of conversion (instead of defining an ENUM_UTILS_DEFINE_CONVERSION() macro for each of the items of E).
